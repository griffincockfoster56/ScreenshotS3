Screenshot S3 — Launch Tweet Thread
=====================================

--- Tweet 1 ---

Just shipped Screenshot S3 — a tiny macOS menu bar app that automatically uploads every screenshot you take to an S3 bucket and copies the link to your clipboard.

Free, open source, built in a weekend with Claude Code.

https://www.screenshots3.xyz

--- Tweet 2 ---

Why I built this: I'm constantly sending screenshots to Claude Code in the terminal. Having a way to instantly get a URL for any screenshot I take — without leaving the command line — is a game changer.

Take screenshot → link on clipboard → paste it anywhere. That's it.

--- Tweet 3 ---

Setup takes 2 minutes:

1. Input your AWS access key + secret key
2. We'll create a new S3 bucket for you, or you can use an existing one

Every screenshot you take from now on gets uploaded automatically and the link lands on your clipboard.

--- Tweet 4 ---

Security was important to me:

- Your AWS keys never leave your machine
- Credentials go directly to AWS — no middleman server
- Zero telemetry or analytics
- No third-party dependencies
- 100% open source — read every line yourself

--- Tweet 5 ---

It also has a built-in gallery so you can browse all your past uploads, re-copy links, or open them in your browser.

All from the menu bar. No Dock icon, no windows cluttering your workspace.

--- Tweet 6 ---

The whole thing is a SwiftUI app with zero external dependencies. Just Apple frameworks + AWS S3.

Check it out, it's completely free:
